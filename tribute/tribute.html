MOTHER TERESA

1910 - 1997

Saint Teresa Of Calcutta

Mother Teresa was an exceptional lady. Her real name was Agnes Conxha Bojaxhiu and mother Teresa born on 26th August 1910 in Skopje (Serbia) and started her career as a teacher and appointed as a geography teacher in Mary’s High School, Calcutta (Kolkata) By dint of her ability, hard work, and service she rose to the rank of headmistress in the same school. Once she had gone to Darjeeling on vacations. From the railway window, she saw half-naked ailing men, women, and children who were leading a worm-like life. She realized that it made her for this neglect. It became the aim of her life with awarded Noble prize for service. and Mother Teresa was the greatest servant of humanity and the ray of hope for the suffering and helpless humanity and goddess incarnate and dies on 5th Sept 1997 in Calcutta.In 2016, Mother Teresa was canonised by the Roman Catholic Church as Saint Teresa.
